About This Home

Immaculate paired home in the heart of Stapleton. Bright open concept layout with 9’ ceilings and beautiful wide plank hardwood floors. Spacious open kitchen boasts solid Corian counter tops, high end Whirlpool stainless steel appliances to include a 5 burner range, large island and… breakfast bar and a pantry with ample storage space. Cozy loft offering great additional living space. Master retreat has a 5 piece en suite bath and a walk-in closet. Bonus features include: Energy Star built home, new Lennox A/C unit, whole home humidifier, built-in speakers throughout home, tank-less water heater, second story laundry, an attached 2 car garage and a nicely landscaped side yard with raised garden bed and irrigation system. Conveniently located to shopping, restaurants and entertainment.
